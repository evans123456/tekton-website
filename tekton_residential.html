Tekton Residential
At Tekton it is our belief that building your dream home should not be a nightmare and we therefore strive to provide bespoke construction solutions that are highly customizable, affordable, sustainable, technologically sound and offer the best quality standards by taking up the role of Main Contractor and also providing design inputs and construction solutions as value-add services to our clients. When our customer needs to build, we design the house, cost it and help them build it to completion. This helps ease the construction process, is easier on the client’s cash flow and ease of mind. Our emphasis on clear communication and follow-through procedures ensures the client’s objectives are top priority in the planning and execution of all our process. Tekton Mining and Construction was founded on the primary need to provide construction solutions and make the world a better place to live in. We are in the business of building dreams and making them a reality and we believe in the delivery of quality works, within the client’s budget and in a timely manner. We offer a wide range of services from Design, costing, construction and development, project management, renovations and repairs etc. We seek to offer our clients a one step, hustle-fee hub where they can come and find lasting solutions to achieve their goals. We strive to make sure our clients get value for money.

WHY DESIGN AND BUILD
In Design & Build, the client signs only ONE Contract and we take care of the rest of the business from Concepts, Design and Construction. Compared to the traditional building process, they save up to 40% of the construction time and savings of cost of up to 20% of traditional approach budget. We have invested tremendous human and capital resources in ensuring that we have a total grasp on all the issues affecting the Building Process. The traditional building approach consumes a lot of time, money and resources that can be optimized by using the Tekton Design & Build Approach. Our Design and Build Model offers our clients a one-stop-shop, peace of mind, and affordable solutions in home ownership We make it easier for the client to enjoy the benefits of their capital investment by working faster and in co-ordination with the entire building team.
