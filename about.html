A Few Words About Us

HISTORY AND PHILOSOPHY

Tekton Mining and Construction Limited was founded on the primary need to provide construction solutions and make the world a better place to live in. We have accumulated the relevant skills, know-how and experiences in design and build solutions, building trades and project management services. We strive to provide bespoke construction solutions that are highly customizable, affordable, sustainable, technologically sound and offer the best quality standards by taking up the role of Main Contractor and also providing design inputs and construction solutions as value-add services to our clients. Our objective is to provide our clients with assured experience when chosen to execute their projects. Our emphasis on clear communication and follow-through procedures ensures the client’s objectives are top priority in the planning and execution of all our processes. We believe the success of any project is based on people, process and product. Consequently, our management belief is built on integrity, quality and service along with a positive attitude, an aptitude for strategic thought, planning and the ability to adapt quickly to new ideas and situations.

What We Do

We believe in the delivery of quality works, within the client’s budget and in a timely manner. As Tekton, we are in the business of building dreams and making them a reality. We offer a wide range of services from Design, costing, construction and development, project management, renovations and repairs, mining and logistics.
